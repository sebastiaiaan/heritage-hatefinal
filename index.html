Heritage, Hate, and Genocide

~ Heritage & Hate - Final Project ~

An Introduction to the Website

This website aims to explore heritage, hate, and genocide with a focus on the regions of the American South and Southeastern Europe.

The website concludes with an interpreation of the connection between all three topics, providing a guiding framework for the future. Readers should be left with many ideas to contemplate and a motivation to change the world.

We hope that through this project we can spread awareness of the issues and their causes for all three of these topics.

Motivation Behind the Project

This project was greatly motivated by the ongoing conflict between Israel and Palestine, specifically by the recent attacks on civilians.

Throughout the course, we were exposed to genocides that occurred in history, of which some were widely unknown. We wanted to create a project that brought these genocides to light.

Ultimately, we were motivated to create something that might create a deeper understanding of heritage and hate, as we believe they are the impetus of genocide.

To begin, start by clicking on "Heritage" in the navigation bar.
